1. What is the SSC GPA Calculator?

The SSC GPA Calculator is an online tool designed to help students in Bangladesh calculate their Grade Point Average (GPA) based on SSC exam results. It simplifies the complex grading system and gives instant results.

2. How does the GPA system work in SSC?

GPA (Grade Point Average) is calculated based on the grade points earned in each subject. The highest grade point is 5.00 for marks between 80 and 100. The final GPA is the average of all subject grade points, excluding the optional subject bonus.

3. Do I need to include my optional subject in GPA calculation?

Yes, but the optional subject only contributes if its grade point is higher than 2. If so, the extra points (grade point ‚àí 2) are added to your total GPA as a bonus.
